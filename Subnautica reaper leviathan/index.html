Biology

The Reaper Leviathan is a creature from the video game series of Subnautica. It is a giant leviathan class lifeform reaching up to 55 meters long. It has red fins, very little bioluminescence, four big black eyes, and four giant black mandibles. It has two pectoral fins running along the top of its body and two ventral fins on its belly. It's head has a horn that seems to be used to ram into things, maybe even other Reaper Leviathans or other Leviathans.

Image of a Reaper Leviathan

Behavior

The Reaper Leviathan is one of the few blind creatures in the Subnautica world. It uses echolocation by roaring to know where everything is. It's roar can be heard from very long distances and if you can hear it's roar, it can see you. It usally will circle it's prey before attacking it. If the Reaper Leviathan gets hurt, it will swim away and roar for a brief second and then come back to attack again.

Abilities

The Reaper Leviathan has the ability to hold people in its mandibles and swallow them whole. Also it has the ability to crush submersibles able to withstand the pressure of 900 meters below sea level. It also will drag people or submersibles down to the sea bed, destroying the submersibles and killing the person.
